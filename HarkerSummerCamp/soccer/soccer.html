What is soccer?

In many places of the world , soccer is known as football. Only in America and Australia it is called soccer. It is the most popular sport in the world, as it is played in more than 200 countries around the world. Approximately 250 million people play soccer every year and it has been an olympic sport for around 120 years! No one really knows when soccer was created but the earliest versions of the game can be traced back more than 3,ooo years ago. Soccer is a teamsport of two teams that each consist of 11 people. They play with a spherical ball that is shot into goals on either side of the field. The object of the game is to move the ball beyond the goal line to get a point. The team with most points at the end of the match wins.
